# 📼 Cassette Tape App Plan

## Concept
A retro cassette tape music app focused on intentional friction and slow listening.

> Not convenience, but experience.

## Core Features
- A/B Side (30min each)
- Noise between tracks
- Manual flip interaction
- Time-limited tape system

## Philosophy
- Intentional inconvenience
- Slow listening
- Physical constraints in digital

## UX Highlights
- No easy skipping
- Noise delays
- Flip to continue
- Tape-based time visualization

## Goal
Users don’t skip music. They listen.
